Summer and Friends

The Best Season of the Year, With the Best People

Summer is my favorite season of the year, not only because my birthday falls right in the middle of it, but because all of my friends are home from school, there's summer baseball games and concerts, the weather is great, and we are exam and homework free! Because I live so close to a lake where all my friends have boats, I spend most of my time (when I'm not working) tubing, jet skiing, and just relaxing in the temporarily beautiful weather.
